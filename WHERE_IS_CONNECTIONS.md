## рҹ”ҙ The Red Badge

The **RED NUMBER BADGE** shows:
- How many people want to connect with you
- Updates automatically every 30 seconds
- Disappears when you have no pending requests

**Example:**
- `Connections 3` = 3 people waiting for your response
- `Connections 5` = 5 people waiting
- `Connections` = No pending requests

## рҹ“Ӣ What's Inside?

When you click "Connections", you'll see:

### Tab 1: Requests
- People who want to connect with you
- **Accept** or **Reject** buttons
- See their name, blood type, donations

### Tab 2: Connected
- People you're already connected with
- See their phone numbers
- **Call** or **Remove** buttons
